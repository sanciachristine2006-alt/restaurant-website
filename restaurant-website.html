Our Story

Passion on Every Plate

Born from a love of French culinary tradition and a desire to push boundaries, Maison Éclat opened its doors in 2018 with a singular vision: to transform the act of dining into a truly extraordinary experience.

Every dish tells a story. We source our ingredients from local farms and trusted suppliers, ensuring that each plate reflects the season's finest offerings combined with techniques honed across decades of culinary excellence.

Chef Laurent Dubois

Executive Chef & Co-Founder

Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Do you cater to dietary requirements and allergies? +
Absolutely. We accommodate all dietary requirements including vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, nut-free, and dairy-free menus. Please inform us when booking and our chef will create a bespoke menu for your needs.
How far in advance should I book? +
We recommend booking at least 2–3 weeks in advance, especially for weekend dinners and special occasions. For same-week reservations, please call us directly as cancellations occasionally free up spots.
Is there a dress code? +
We kindly request smart casual to formal attire. We ask that guests avoid sportswear, shorts, and flip-flops to maintain the ambiance we've created for all our guests.
Do you offer private dining for events? +
Yes! Our private dining room accommodates up to 30 guests and can be booked for birthdays, corporate dinners, anniversaries, and other celebrations. Contact us for bespoke menus and pricing.
Is parking available? +
Complimentary valet parking is available from 6:00 PM daily. During lunch, street parking is available on adjacent roads, and there is a public car park two minutes' walk away.
What is your cancellation policy? +
We request 24 hours' notice for cancellations. For parties of 6 or more, a 48-hour notice is required. Late cancellations or no-shows may incur a small per-person charge.
